# AI Retrofit Check — Business Context

## Product purpose

AI Retrofit Check helps small and medium-sized companies identify where AI and automation can create practical value before they invest in disconnected tools or expensive implementation projects.

The product turns a structured business assessment into a clear AI-readiness analysis, recommended first sprint, and 30-day roadmap.

## Target users

- SME owners and managing directors
- Operations managers
- Finance and administration leaders
- Professional-service firms
- Novixx consultants and commercial partners

## Main business problem

Many SMEs know they should use AI, but they do not know:

- which processes should be improved first
- whether their data and systems are ready
- which tools fit their real workflows
- where risks, fragmented processes, or manual work create the highest cost
- how to turn AI interest into an actionable first project

## Proposed solution

A web-based assessment dashboard where a company answers structured questions about its processes, systems, data, team, and priorities.

The platform generates:

- AI readiness score
- process and data maturity snapshot
- identified bottlenecks
- recommended first automation or AI sprint
- 30-day roadmap
- downloadable report
- option to request a Novixx consultation

## Business value

For SMEs:
- clearer priorities before investing in AI
- lower risk of choosing the wrong tools
- practical roadmap instead of generic AI advice

For Novixx:
- scalable lead-generation product
- structured client discovery process
- stronger basis for consulting and implementation proposals

## Scope for version 1

- questionnaire and assessment logic
- scoring engine
- AI-generated recommendations
- dashboard with results
- PDF report generation
- lead capture and consultation request

## Out of scope for version 1

- autonomous AI agents acting inside client systems
- direct ERP or accounting-system integrations
- automated execution of workflows
- enterprise multi-tenant administration
